BARRIO BREWING CO.

WEBSITE 520-791-2739 800 E 16th St,

Barrio Brewing Co. is a beloved Tucson brewery offering a wide selection of craft beers and delicious pub fare. Located in a historic building, Barrio Brewing Co. provides a cozy atmosphere where patrons can enjoy great company and even better brews. Whether you're a beer enthusiast or just looking for a relaxing hangout spot, Barrio Brewing Co. has something for everyone.
