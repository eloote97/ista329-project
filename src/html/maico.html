MAICO'S MEXICAN RESTAURANT

WEBSITE (520)-294-2836 835 E 22nd St

Maico's Mexican Restaurant brings the vibrant flavors of Mexico to Tucson with its authentic dishes and warm hospitality. From savory tacos to hearty enchiladas, each dish is prepared with care and traditional Mexican spices. Whether you're dining in for a family meal or grabbing takeout for a quick bite, Maico's Mexican Restaurant offers a taste of home-cooked Mexican cuisine.
