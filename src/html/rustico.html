TACOS RUSTICO

WEBSITE (520) 623-3478 2281 N Oracle Rd

Tacos Rustico is a cozy taco spot located in the heart of Tucson. Known for their authentic flavors and fresh ingredients, Tacos Rustico offers a variety of tacos, burritos, and other Mexican delights. Whether you're craving carne asada, al pastor, or vegetarian options, Tacos Rustico has something to satisfy every palate.
